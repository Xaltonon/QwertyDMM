<b><h2>Standard Keybinds</h2></b><br/>
<p>
<b>CTRL+S:</b> Save<br/>
<b>CTRL+Z:</b> Undo<br/>
<b>CTRL+SHIFT+Z:</b> Redo<br/>
<b>CTRL+Y:</b> Redo
</p><br/><br/>

<b><h2>Movement Keybinds</h2></b><br/>
<p>
<b>Arrow Keys:</b> Pan<br/>
<b>SHIFT+Arrow Keys:</b> Pan Pages<br/>
<b>ALT+CLICK+DRAG:</b> Pan<br/>
<b>MMB+DRAG:</b> Pan<br/>
<b>Mouse Wheel:</b> Zoom
</p><br/><br/>


<b><h2>Mode Keybinds</h2></b><br/>
<p>
<b>SHIFT+A:</b> Placement Mode<br/>
<b>SHIFT+S:</b> Select Mode<br/>
<b>SHIFT+D:</b> Delete Mode
</p><br/>

<b><h3>Placement Mode</h3></b><br/>
<p>
<b>SHIFT+CLICK+DRAG:</b> Block Placement Mode<br/>
<b>CTRL+CLICK+DRAG:</b> Directional Placement Mode<br/>
<b>CTRL+SHIFT+CLICK+DRAG:</b> Pixel Placement Mode
</p><br/>

<b><h3>Delete Mode</h3></b><br/>
<p>
<b>SHIFT+CLICK+DRAG:</b> Block Deletion<br/>
</p><br/>
